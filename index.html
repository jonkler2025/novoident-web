What is Chat Control?

Chat Control is the EU’s plan to scan every private message, photo, and file. It means mass surveillance of innocent people — treating every citizen like a suspect.

Why it’s Dangerous

  • Violates the right to privacy and free expression
  • Creates dangerous backdoors in encryption
  • Puts journalists, activists, and ordinary people at risk
  • Sets a precedent for authoritarian surveillance
